About Our Therapy Center

Meet Perminas, Our Founder & Lead Therapist

Reliable Occupational Therapy Service

My name is Perminas, and I am the founder of Special Needs Therapy Hub, a trusted therapy center based in Nairobi, Kenya. With a deep commitment to helping individuals with special needs reach their full potential, we provide comprehensive, evidence-based therapeutic services.

Our dedicated team consists of experienced, compassionate professionals committed to empowering every client. We specialize in pediatric occupational therapy, speech therapy, and behavioral interventions, taking a holistic approach that addresses the physical, cognitive, emotional, and social aspects of development.

Specialized Therapeutic Interventions

Comprehensive programs designed to address each child's unique challenges and strengths

Autism Spectrum Therapy

Our evidence-based interventions for children with autism include:

  • Sensory integration therapy
  • Social skills development
  • Behavioral modification strategies
  • Communication enhancement
  • Daily living skills training
Learn More

ADHD & Focus Support

Specialized programs to help children with attention challenges:

  • Executive function training
  • Organizational skill development
  • Sensory strategies for focus
  • Impulse control techniques
  • Classroom accommodation planning
Learn More

Developmental Milestones

Early intervention for developmental delays:

  • Gross and fine motor skill development
  • Cognitive and perceptual training
  • Feeding and oral motor therapy
  • Parent coaching and education
  • Play-based learning strategies
Learn More

Stroke Rehabilitation

Comprehensive post-stroke recovery programs:

  • Upper extremity rehabilitation
  • Cognitive retraining
  • Activities of daily living (ADL) training
  • Adaptive equipment assessment
  • Home modification recommendations
Learn More

Cerebral Palsy Support

Specialized interventions for children with CP:

  • Motor function improvement
  • Positioning and seating assessments
  • Assistive technology training
  • Pain management strategies
  • Transition planning
Learn More

School-Based Services

Support for educational success:

  • IEP development support
  • Teacher training and consultation
  • Classroom adaptation strategies
  • Handwriting without tears program
  • Sensory-friendly classroom design
